Inset Architectural Relief

Forms that emerge from within the wall.

Liminal Vault Studio creates inset architectural reliefs. Sculptural forms fabricated off-site and installed directly into existing walls. Each piece is designed for its space, seated with precision, and finished to feel discovered rather than applied.

What We Make

Inset architectural reliefs are sculptural wall works designed to integrate seamlessly into existing architecture. Each piece is custom measured, fabricated in the studio, and installed in a single controlled operation.

Inset Relief

Pre-fabricated relief seated into a measured wall opening and finished to integrate with the surrounding surface.

Sculptural Installations

Larger works designed for interiors where scale and spatial presence are central. Built in sections when needed.

Relief Panels

Smaller scale pieces for clients seeking the language of inset relief without a full custom build.

How It Works

1. Site Visit

Measure, photograph, and understand the space, light, and wall conditions.

2. Studio Fabrication

All sculptural work is fabricated off-site under controlled conditions for precision and consistency.

3. Installation

Remove a measured drywall section, seat the completed relief, secure, seal, and finish for seamless integration.

No on-site sculpting, carving, or heavy material work is performed inside the home.
