Inset Architectural Relief

Forms that emerge
from within the wall.

Liminal Vault Studio creates inset architectural reliefs fabricated off-site and installed directly into existing walls. Each piece is designed to feel discovered rather than applied.

Private commissions are accepted on a limited basis and scheduled according to studio availability.

What We Make

Inset architectural reliefs are sculptural wall works designed to integrate seamlessly into existing architecture.

Inset Relief: Pre-fabricated relief seated into a measured wall opening.

Sculptural Installations: Larger works built in sections when required.

Relief Panels: Smaller works using the same architectural language.

How It Works

1. Site Visit: Measure, photograph, and assess the space.

2. Studio Fabrication: All sculpting is completed off-site.

3. Installation: Wall opening, seating, sealing, and finish.

No on-site sculpting or heavy material work inside the home.
